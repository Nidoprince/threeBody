Move with wasd. Enter or exit ship with e. Park or unpark with p. Mine with m when touching an asteroid and in a spaceship or when touching a fuel deposit on a planet outside a spaceship.

Build new things from the menu which can be opened with b. Select with arrows or wasd, then use enter to build from the menu. Towing ship may attach or release a tow line on a nearby ship with t.

Control camera with arrows. Focus character with space. Lock or unlock focus with f. Zoom in and out with [ and ]. Reset Zoom with \. Toggle velocity info with v.
